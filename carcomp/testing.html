BRIEF HISTORY

Maasai Mara university is a public university which was awarded a university charter on the year 2013 April 25th.It was formely a Branch of Moi university called Narok university college before changing the name to Maasai Mara University.Due to our better perfomance we have been able to improve in our academics to a top level institution which is top ten in university ranking.We are also proud to partner with international organizations which have been able to give full scholarship to our students who performs well in both academics and sports.
We as Maasai Mara community wishes to thank you all for your continued support and welcomes you to join us and work together.We welcome all new students!We are happy to have you in this journey.
Thank you!
